CROHN'S DISEASE

It affects any part of GI tract that is from the mouth to the anus causing inflammation of multiple layers of the walls.Most often it affects the portion of small intestine.It damages the area next to healthy tissues.

SYMPTOMS

Below mentioned symptoms can range from mild to severe.

  • Diarrhea
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Mouth sores
  • Loss of Appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anemia
  • pain near anus
  • inflammation of liver, skin, eyes and joints

If symptoms mentioned above continuous then You need to See A Doctor.

CAUSES

Exact cause is unknown but possible cause is malfunction of immune system.An abnormal response to foreign organisms causes the immune system to attack the cells in the digestive tract.

Heredity is common factor in causing Crohn's disease.

Smoking is most important risk factor for Crohn's disease.

Future Complications

  • Bowel Obstruction, affecting the thickness of intestinal wall and blocking flow of digestive content.
  • Ulcers, anywhere on digestive tract including mouth and anus.
  • Fistulas between intestine and skin, in abdomen, into bladder or vagina and soon.
  • Anal Fissure, is small tears in tissue or around skin that lines the anus.
  • Bone loss(Osteoporosis)
  • Malnutrition

Foods to Avoid

  • alcohol (mixed drinks, beer, wine)
  • butter, mayonnaise, margarine, oils
  • carbonated beverages
  • coffee, tea, chocolate, sugar
  • dairy products (if lactose intolerant)
  • gas-producing foods (lentils, beans, legumes, cabbage, broccoli, onions)
  • nuts and seeds (peanut butter, other nut butters)
  • foods high in fiber
  • whole grains and bran
  • spicy foods
  • fatty foods (fried foods)
  • raw fruits, vegetables
  • red meat and pork

Food To Enjoy

  • Plain crackers
  • Eggs
  • Tofu
  • Banana and Cantalope
  • Aspargus tips, mushroom and Squash
  • Yogurt, kimchi, miso, sauerkraut, and tempeh
  • White rice
  • White bread without seeds
  • cooked vegetables without skins or seeds
  • Lean Meat
  • Oily Fish
  • Green Tea
  • Oatmeal
  • Turmeric

Dietary Measures to Take

  • Eat small meals (rather then 2-3 large meal have 5-6 small meals)
  • Food rich in thiamine, iron, folate and vitmain A,C,D
  • Drink Plenty of water(8-10 glass per day)
  • Low fiber rich fruits peeled or poached
  • Do weight-bearing exercise for joints,moderate aerobic exercise, moderate pace walk, resistance training and yoga to relieve stress.
