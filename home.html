INFLAMMATORY BOWEL DISEASE (IBD)

IBD is a term for Ulcerative Colitis and Crohn's Disease which are characterized by prolonged inflammation and chronic inflammation of gastrointestinal(GI) tract.

SYMPTOMS OF IBD

  • Abdominal Pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Weight Loss
  • Fatigue
  • Fever

CAUSES OF IBD

Exact cause is unknown but it is the result of defective immune system. A good immune system attacks foreign organisms such as bacteria and viruses to protect body.In IBD, the immune system responds incorrectly to environmental triggers causing inflammation of the GI tract.

COMMON TYPES of IBD

Ulcerative Colitis

It occurs in the large intestine(colon) and the rectum causing redness and swelling oin the innermost layer and damaging the healthy tissues .It can last for few weeks or for several months.

Crohn's Disease

It affects any part of GI tract that is from the mouth to the anus causing inflammation of multiple layers of the walls.Most often it affects the portion of small intestine.It damages the area next to healthy tissues.
