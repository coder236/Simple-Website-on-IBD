ULCERATIVE COLITIS

It occurs in the large intestine(colon) and the rectum causing redness and swelling oin the innermost layer and damaging the healthy tissues .It can last for few weeks or for several months.

SYMPTOMS

If below mentioned symptoms may come and go up to a year, thus these occurrences are called flare-ups.

  • Diarrhea
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Urgency to defecate
  • Loss of Appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Sleep issues
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Anemia

If symptoms mentioned above continuous then You need to See A Doctor.

CAUSES

Exact cause is unknown but possible cause is Malfunction of immune system.An abnormal response to foreign organisms causes the immune system to attack the cells in the digestive tract.

Although Stress doesn't cause inflammatory bowel disease but it can make your signs and symptoms worse and may trigger flare-ups.

Heredity is also one of the common role in causing ulcerative colitis.

Future Complications

  • Severe bleeding
  • A hole in the colon(Perforated colon)
  • Severe dehydration
  • Liver Disease
  • Bone loss(Osteoporosis)
  • Inflammation of skin, joints and eyes
  • An increased risk of colon cancer
  • Increased risk of blood clots in veins and ateries

Foods to Avoid

  • Limit Dairy Products
  • Limit high Fiber foods like
    • fruits such as berries, pears, melon and oranges
    • vegetables such as broccoli, cauliflower, peas, beans, pluses, carrots, potatoes with skin and sweetcorns
    • whole grains such as barley, rye, cereals, oats, bread
    • nuts and seeds
  • Limit spicy food
  • Limit Alcohol
  • Limit Caffeine

Dietary Measures to Take

  • Do Exercises like Yoga, Relaxation techinques,Brisk walk
  • Eat small meals (rather then 2-3 large meal have 5-6 small meals)
  • Drink Plenty of water(8-10 glass per day)
  • When preparing food stick to methods like boiling, grilling,steaming or poaching
  • Eat food rich in potassium, folate, magnesium, calcium, iron, probiotics and low-fibre
